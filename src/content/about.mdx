# What I Do

I've been writing code professionally for over a decade, working on everything from small startups to large-scale distributed systems. My focus is on practical, production-ready solutions that solve real problems.

Through this blog and my YouTube channel, I share what I've learned building and maintaining systems at scale. Each article is designed to teach not just the "how" but also the "why" behind technical decisions.

Topics I cover include:

- Modern web development with React, TypeScript, and Node.js
- DevOps practices, CI/CD pipelines, and infrastructure automation
- Database design, optimization, and scaling strategies
- Container orchestration with Kubernetes and Docker
- System design and software architecture patterns
